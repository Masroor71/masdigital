About

My name is Masroor, but my friends call me Mas or Mash! I’m a Google UX Design Professional with 2+ years of experience working with various international clients. I am a Master of Technology Entrepreneurship and Innovation (MTEI) graduate from Saint Mary’s University. I’m fortunate to call Halifax my second home, a home that is thousands of miles away from home.

View My Works

Before coming to Halifax, I studied BSc in Economics at North South University (NSU), one of the country's most prestigious business schools. During my time at NSU, I’ve worked on a few economics research projects and successfully published a research paper titled Social Business: Turning Aside from Theoretical Concepts? in the International Journal of Management Sciences & Business Research. 

However, with time I realized I’m more of a person who likes to get out and create opportunities. So, at school, I initiated several social projects, most notably Sip of Life and Solar tiles. Sip of Life was a social business-modeled coffee shop where the profit would go directly to the student medical fund. 

Inspired by Elon Musk’s SolarCity, I tried producing solar tiles specific to Bangladesh's weather conditions that aimed to create a peer-to-peer electricity-sharing economy. Funny enough, I wrote the patent application after realizing the high cost of a patent lawyer. I had a time of life working on this project. These different projects have tremendously helped me develop my critical thinking ability and business development skills. 

After finishing School, I launched a start-up, Skipissues , that aimed to simplify international student admission successfully. As a Co-founder of Product & Digital Strategy, I gained significant experience in digital marketing, product development, and business strategy. In addition, I had the opportunity to build the website from scratch while simultaneously formulating a digital marketing strategy, working with different schools, and helping students succeed in the admission process.

I also had the opportunity to work with various international clients as a product strategist.

While working at Skipissues, I realized the importance of specialized knowledge of entrepreneurship. I was fortunate to get accepted into the MTEI program at SMU . My experiences have made me a confident business leader with a deep passion for technology innovation and product marketing.

In my free time, I dream quite a bit - from being a seafarer to a monk living secluded in some place in the world. I love playing badminton & soccer with my friends. And, when I’m doing nothing, which doesn't happen often, I wonder about life - A life full of potential, filled with many if, but, how, where, and why! 

Thank you.
